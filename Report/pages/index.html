Extract, Transform, and Load

Overview


In this project we gathered data to create a database that could be used to examine relationships of factors to car accidents in New York State. Some possible analysis that may be performed using this data may explore:

  • Does weather play a role in the time it takes to clear an accident?
  • Do demographics and population compared to wealth across counties in NY?
  • Is the time of day related to number of accidents around certain infrastructure features?

Links to the data sources and process are provided in the upper right corner.


Limitations


We constrained the data in our database to reflect only accidents involving cars in New York State from 2016 to 2020. Census data is from 2016 to 2019 as the Bureau has yet to release 2020. Population data is from the seven counties intersecting with New York City:

  • New York County
  • Kings County
  • Queens County
  • Suffolk County
  • Bronx County
  • Richmond County
  • Nassau County

Database


We used AWS to host a SQL relational database that houses our data.
